Problem Statement

Air pollution negatively affects the urban ecosystem, damaging vegetation, reducing biodiversity, and contributing to climate change. Poor air quality has significant impacts on public health, contributing to respiratory diseases, cardiovascular issues, and reduced quality of life.

Why is this Problem Important?

Air pollution is a major issue because it directly impacts public health, causing respiratory and cardiovascular diseases, increasing healthcare costs, and reducing productivity.
